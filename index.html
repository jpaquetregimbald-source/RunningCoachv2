Phase 1Threshold Foundation · Wks 1–3
Objective: Re-establish threshold tolerance. Build your Z2 aerobic base — the foundation everything else is built on.

If 7 km feels too easy — good. This base sustains Z4 for 5 km in Week 10.

Speed-Endurance Mix
